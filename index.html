Our Story

Born from Passion,
Refined by Time

Amain Unisex Salon was founded in 2020 by South Sundanese-trained stylist Amani Justine James after almost a decade of working in haute couture beauty circles in Uganda and Sundan. Her philosophy: every person who walks through our doors deserves a transformative experience, not just a service.
